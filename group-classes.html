Group Classes

Experience the energy and motivation of working out together! Our group classes are designed for all fitness levels and led by certified instructors who make every session fun, challenging, and rewarding.

Why Join Group Classes?

  • High-energy, social workouts
  • Wide variety of class types
  • Motivating and supportive environment

Group Class Etiquette & Tips

  • Arrive on time and be ready to move
  • Bring a towel and water bottle
  • Respect your classmates and instructor
  • Let the instructor know if you have injuries or limitations
  • Have fun and encourage others!

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to sign up in advance?

We recommend booking your spot online, but walk-ins are welcome if space allows.

What should I bring?

Comfortable workout clothes, a towel, and a water bottle.

Are classes suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! Our instructors offer modifications for all fitness levels.
